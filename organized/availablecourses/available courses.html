BLOCK CHAIN

Blockchain is a shared, immutable digital ledger, enabling the recording of transactions and the tracking of assets within a business network and providing a single source of truth. Blockchain operates as a decentralized distributed database, with data stored across multiple computers, making it resistant to tampering.
